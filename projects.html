Sales Analysis with Pandas and Matplotlib

This project delved into a year's sales data to uncover trends and customer behaviors, utilizing Pandas for data integration and cleaning, and Matplotlib for visualization. Through data from 12 CSV files, it aimed to answer critical business questions to inform data-driven decisions.

Insights included peak sales periods, cities with the highest sales, and the most effective times for advertisements. The analysis also revealed popular product combinations and top-selling items, providing a basis for strategic planning.

Walmart Sales Analysis

This initiative sought to delve deep into Walmart's vast sales datasets to uncover key insights into store performance, product line success, and customer purchasing behaviors. Through comprehensive data analysis, the project aimed to spotlight areas of strength and opportunity within Walmart's sales strategy.

The analysis spanned product performance, sales patterns, and customer segmentation, utilizing data wrangling techniques for data integrity and exploratory data analysis to address a series of pivotal questions.

Key findings from the project provided Walmart with actionable insights into optimizing product lines, tailoring sales strategies, and enhancing customer engagement strategies. This project demonstrates the power of data in shaping retail strategies and customer experiences.
